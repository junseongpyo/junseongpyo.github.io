Research Interests

I'm interested in smart home agents and multimodal AI grounded in speech and spoken language.

Smart Home Agents.

The home is the space where people spend most of their lives — yet it remains one of the least explored frontiers in AI research. Home environments are dynamic and personal, accumulating time-series context that agents must reason over — not just respond to immediate commands. I work on benchmarks and evaluation frameworks that reveal where current LLM agents fall short in this setting.

Speech and Spoken Language.

Most AI systems treat language as text, discarding the richness of how people actually speak. I'm interested in grounding agents in spoken interaction, where prosody, disfluency, and speaker identity carry meaning beyond the words.
